🔒 Privacy Policy

Your data is as sacred as a family recipe — minimal, respected, and never exploited.

📅 Effective: March 24, 2026 | Last Updated: March 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Orvynza Flavor Loom ("we", "us", or "the Platform"), your privacy is a cornerstone of our culinary philosophy. We believe that exploring recipes, techniques, and gastronomic stories should never come at the cost of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website, you agree to the practices described herein.

We operate under a minimal-data-first approach — we don’t sell your data, we don’t run intrusive ad networks, and we never share personal information for profit. The Flavor Loom is a sanctuary, and we protect it accordingly.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data (Server Logs & Analytics)

When you visit Orvynza Flavor Loom, our servers may automatically collect standard technical information such as your IP address (anonymized where possible), browser type, operating system, referring pages, date/time stamps, and general usage patterns. We use lightweight, privacy-friendly analytics (no external tracking giants) to understand which recipes resonate most and to improve site performance. This data is never sold or used for cross-site advertising.

2.2 Information You Provide Voluntarily

We only collect personal information when you actively choose to share it. This includes:

  • Contact Forms: If you reach out via hello@orvynzaflavorloom.com or our contact widget, we may receive your name, email address, and message content. We use this solely to respond to inquiries.
  • Comments or feedback: If you submit a recipe review or comment (future feature), we may store your display name and comment. You are never required to provide real names.
  • Newsletter (optional): If you opt into our mailing list (when available), we collect your email to share seasonal recipes and updates — you can unsubscribe anytime.
🍪 Cookie-Light Pledge: We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising or retargeting. We only use essential session cookies to maintain site functionality and optional preference cookies. No third-party ad trackers, no Facebook pixels, no Google Analytics invasive scripts. Your browsing stays inside the Loom.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • ✓ To provide, maintain, and improve the Flavor Loom experience (e.g., fixing bugs, optimizing recipe discovery).
  • ✓ To respond to your messages, recipe requests, or partnership inquiries.
  • ✓ To send occasional updates if you explicitly opt in (newsletter). No spam, ever.
  • ✓ To protect the security and integrity of our website against fraud or abuse.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making, profiling, or any form of behavioral advertising. Your culinary journey remains yours.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Orvynza Flavor Loom respects your privacy and does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Limited sharing occurs only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., secure email hosting, minimalist analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements. They are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights, property, or safety of Orvynza, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify you via prominent notice on the site.

We never share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience. Here's the breakdown:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site navigation, security, and basic functionality. No consent needed as they are strictly necessary.
  • Preference Cookies: If you adjust theme settings (future pink/black mode) or dismiss notifications, a lightweight cookie remembers your choice.
  • No Marketing Cookies: We do not deploy cookies for advertising, cross-site tracking, or remarketing.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some parts of the site may not function optimally. For more details, see our Cookie Information.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Contact form inquiries are stored for up to 24 months for follow-up purposes; newsletter emails remain until you unsubscribe. We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, firewalls, restricted access) to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure — you share information at your own risk.

If you ever wish to request deletion of your data (e.g., contact history), please email privacy@orvynzaflavorloom.com and we will honor your request within 30 days.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (including GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@orvynzaflavorloom.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy rights. You may also opt out of any non-essential communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

8. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

Our recipe pages may occasionally include links to external ingredient sources, culinary tools, or reference articles. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information. Embedded videos (e.g., from Vimeo) may set their own cookies if you interact with them; we choose privacy-conscious embeds where possible.

9. Children's Privacy

Orvynza Flavor Loom is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has submitted information may contact us for removal.

10. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access Orvynza from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer. We ensure that any international data handling complies with applicable data protection laws, including standard contractual clauses where required.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Effective Date" and posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. If we make material changes, we will provide a notice on the homepage or via email (if you are subscribed). Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
